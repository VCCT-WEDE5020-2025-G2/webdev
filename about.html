MEET THE OWNER

G-TECH was founded by Aaron Warner, a tech enthusiast with over 15 years of experience in the industry. John's passion for technology and commitment to customer service has been the driving force behind G-TECH's success. Under his leadership, G-TECH has grown from a small repair shop to a trusted name in the tech community, known for its reliable services and high-quality refurbished products. John's vision is to make technology accessible and affordable for everyone, while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer care.
